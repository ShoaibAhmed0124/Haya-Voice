HAYA Animation Engine Video Assets Folder
=========================================

To enable the HD Live video features of HAYA, place your three video files in this directory:
1. IDLE.mp4
2. TALKING.mp4
3. EXPRESSING.mp4

How to upload files to this directory in AI Studio:
1. In the left-hand sidebar of your AI Studio Code Editor, you will see a file explorer.
2. Locate the folder named "public".
3. Right-click on the "public" folder or hover over it to see options to upload files, OR drag-and-drop the files directly from your computer into the "public" folder in the sidebar.
4. Make sure the filenames match the case and naming exactly:
   - IDLE.mp4
   - TALKING.mp4
   - EXPRESSING.mp4

Once the files are uploaded, the fallback Vector HUD mode can be toggled to "HD Live" mode in the application UI, and HAYA's video engine will start using your high-fidelity video animations!
